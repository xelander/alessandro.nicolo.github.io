Product description

Job Res is an organization that produces IT solutions for small and medium-sized businesses. One of the proposed solutions is the JobRes application, a portal designed to manage the personal data of the personnel and their timesheets.

Go to interviews

Interviews

Having a good number of clients available, JobRes has made available to me various resources to interact with to do interviews on how they expect the application to work. Most users have reported that they have felt frustrated managing their timesheets on paper, and have long wanted an application that can handle them on their own. Other users, on the other hand, already use such applications, but many problems regarding the speed of use and the fact that many of these applications do not have the mobile version.
