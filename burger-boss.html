Product description

BurgerBoss, a small burger shop located in the center of Rome, leader in the creation of tasty burgers. They are compared to works of art. The menu contains many competitive prices for the area and is about to appear at home deliveries to bring their delicacies directly home.

Go to interviews

Interviews

Interviews were conducted on a group of people who are customers of the shop, on other possible users who are unfamiliar with the burger shop and who are not so familiar with the technology. The interviews highlighted the desire of users to have an application that meets their needs, that is fast in responding and that provides the basic functionalities required by the project objectives.

Impact

What I learned

While designing the BurgerBoss app, I learned that the first ideas for the app are only the beginning of the process. Usability studies and peer feedback influenced each iteration of the app’s designs.

Next steps

Conduct another round of usability studies to validate whether the pain points users experienced have been effectively addressed.

Conduct more user research to determine any new areas of need.
